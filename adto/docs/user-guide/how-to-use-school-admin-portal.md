# How to use the school admin portal

## What this feature does

The school admin portal gives principals, school administrators, department heads, faculty leads, coordinators, and viewers school-scoped access to ACE implementation records.

## When to use it

Use it to review school progress, sessions, calendar entries, reports, inventory readiness, evidence, teacher participation, project outputs, and school setup details.

## Before you begin

An admin must create your user profile and link it to one or more schools in **Schools > School User Access**.

## Steps

1. Open **Dashboard** to view your school-scoped implementation progress.
2. Open **Schools** to review setup details, terms, sections, teachers, remarks, and membership context.
3. Open **Calendar** or **ACE Sessions** to review schedules and historical session status.
4. Open **Projects**, **Inventory**, **Media Uploads**, and **Reports** to review outputs, readiness, evidence, and report previews.
5. Ask an admin or facilitator to correct records that should not be edited directly by school users.

## What happens next

School records remain scoped to your explicit school membership. Contact email is only a temporary fallback for older records that do not yet have memberships.

## Tips or reminders

Multiple school users can belong to the same school. A user can also be linked to multiple schools when needed.

## Common questions or issues

If your pages look empty, ask an admin to confirm your school membership is active and linked to the correct school.
