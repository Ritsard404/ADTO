# How to use the shared school calendar

## What this feature does

The shared school calendar shows ACE sessions and school schedules in month, week, day, and list views.

## When to use it

Use it to check upcoming sessions, review completed or cancelled sessions, inspect teacher and facilitator schedules, and view historical sessions after facilitator reassignment.

## Before you begin

Your account controls what you can see. Admins can view all sessions. Facilitators can view current and historical assignment records. School admins can view their school only.

## Steps

1. Open **Calendar** from the sidebar or bottom navigation.
2. Use the status legend to understand scheduled, ongoing, completed, cancelled, rescheduled, draft, and no-report sessions.
3. Filter by school, grade, section, teacher, facilitator, activity, status, or date range.
4. Hover over a session to preview key details.
5. Select a session to open the details drawer.
6. Use the available actions if the session is editable for your role and assignment status.

## What happens next

ADTO shows only sessions inside your authorized scope. Historical records remain visible but are read-only when the assignment is no longer active.

## Tips or reminders

Use list view on phones when the month grid is too dense. Schedule duplication and Excel paste are still handled from the ACE Sessions schedule workbench.

## Common questions or issues

If sessions are missing, clear filters first. If you still cannot see a school, ask an admin to check your assignment history or school-admin email.
