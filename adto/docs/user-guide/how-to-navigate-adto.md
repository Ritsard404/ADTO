# How to navigate the ADTO workspace

## What this feature does

The ADTO workspace gives admins, facilitators, and school users a consistent layout for ACE dashboards, school records, sessions, reports, inventory, media, analytics, and settings.

## When to use it

Use the sidebar to move between daily ACE operations such as checking school progress, reviewing reports, tracking sessions, and monitoring resources.

## Before you begin

Sign in with an active ADTO account. Your available navigation items depend on your role.

## Steps

1. Use the top-left menu on mobile or the left sidebar on desktop.
2. Open Dashboard for the overall ACE implementation snapshot.
3. Use Management for schools, facilitators, and assignments.
4. Use Operations for ACE sessions and reports.
5. Use Resources for inventory and media uploads.
6. Use Insights and System when your role allows access.

## What happens next

ADTO keeps the page structure consistent so each workspace opens with a clear title, short description, and the relevant table or dashboard cards.

## Tips or reminders

Status badges use ACE colors: blue for active work, green for completed or healthy records, orange for pending items, and red for missed or blocked items.

In local testing mode, use the Admin / Facilitator switcher in the top bar to preview role-specific navigation without signing in.

## Common questions or issues

If you cannot see a navigation item, your account role may not have access to that workspace.
